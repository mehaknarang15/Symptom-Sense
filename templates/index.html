About Us

At SymptomSense, we harness AI to make healthcare smarter, more accessible, and engaging. Our platform provides AI-driven symptom analysis, offering preliminary health insights by cross-referencing medical databases. We integrate mental health support with an AI chatbot, mood tracking, and stress management tools. Users can also access medicine information, menstrual tracking, and wellness challenges to stay informed and proactive about their health. Beyond diagnosis, our community-driven approach allows users to share health experiences, while clinics and professionals provide verified insights. We also offer certifications in health education, helping users learn and grow. With features like voice search and location-based clinic recommendations, we ensure accessibility for all, including those with disabilities. Whether you're seeking medical guidance, mental wellness support, or an interactive health community, SymptomSense is your trusted AI-powered healthcare companion.
